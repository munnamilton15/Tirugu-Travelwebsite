Kodachadri Hills

The Crown Jewel of the Western Ghats

A Trekker's Paradise

Nestled in the heart of the Mookambika Wildlife Sanctuary, Kodachadri is a breathtaking mountain peak with dense forests, cascading waterfalls, and panoramic vistas. Declared a natural heritage site, it's renowned for its stunning sunrise and sunset views, diverse flora and fauna, and the historical significance of the Sarvajna Peetha at its summit.

Trek Difficulty

Moderate

Max Altitude

1,343 meters (4,406 ft)

Best Time to Visit

September to February

Key Attractions

Sarvajna Peetha

A small temple at the peak where the revered philosopher Adi Shankaracharya is said to have meditated.

Hidlumane Falls

A series of six beautiful, cascading waterfalls that offer a refreshing break during the trek.

Off-Road Jeep Ride

An exhilarating jeep journey through rugged terrain takes you part of the way up the mountain.

Sample 2-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival and Ascent

  • Morning departure from Shivamogga.
  • Reach the base camp, have lunch.
  • Start the trek, passing Hidlumane Falls.
  • Reach the homestay/campsite near the peak by evening.
  • Dinner and overnight stay.

Day 2: Summit and Departure

  • Early morning trek to the summit for sunrise.
  • Visit Sarvajna Peetha.
  • Descend back to the base camp.
  • Have lunch and depart for Shivamogga.
